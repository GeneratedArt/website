Vision
GeneratedArt envisions a world where the convergence of technology, real-world data, and human creativity leads to revolutionary innovations in various fields. We aspire to be at the forefront of this revolution, transforming the way art is perceived and utilized, from architecture to fashion, design, our urban environment, and even gaming. Our vision is to harness the power of generative art and real-world data to create impactful solutions and inspire a new era of artistic expression that transcends traditional boundaries.
Mission
The mission of GeneratedArt is to protect, promote, and connect the community of algorithmic creators, living data custodians, artists, and collectors. We are committed to elevating the field of generative art, making it accessible and influential across various sectors. Whether it's for the collection of physical and digital art, practical applications in design and architecture, or using art for philanthropic fundraising, we are dedicated to using our platform to foster innovation, collaboration, social impact, and environmental stewardship. We strive to create an ecosystem where creativity meets technology, leading to meaningful contributions to the arts and society.

Rvig

RVig, Richard Vigniel born in 1976 in Lyon, France, is a generative digital artist based in Geneva, Switzerland. He has been active in this field since 1999, utilizing his computer science background. His work creatively blends algorithms and coding to create unique art. RVig's journey in generative art led him to the NFT space with his 2021 project “Flowers”.

Sha22

Sha22, a collaboration between graphic designer Alisha Henry and 3D artist Boris Rabusseau, specializes in merging various graphic styles using digital technologies like VJing and virtual reality. Since 1992, they've been at the forefront of combining traditional and digital art forms, focusing on adapting to modern lifestyles and critically evaluating the impact of digital technologies.

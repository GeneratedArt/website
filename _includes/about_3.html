In the evolving landscape of generative art, decentralized digital contracts on the blockchain are key. These contracts facilitate collaboration among creators, data providers, and stakeholders, enabling real-time code and data contributions. This approach not only streamlines the production of significant art pieces but also ensures the protection of all parties' interests, marking a new era in digital art creation.
01

GENERATIVE INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY SMART CONTRACTS

Generative IP smart contracts on the blockchain streamline ownership rights and management of generative art NFTs. They integrate source code, token supply, creators, data providers, and stakeholders, fostering innovation in the digital realm.
02

Tokenized Generative Renders (TGR)

Generative Art NFTs are unique digital tokens on the blockchain, each representing a distinct variant of a generative art piece. They appeal to art collectors and buyers, offering a one-of-a-kind ownership experience.

Generative Tokenization

Decentralized blockchain contracts are transforming generative art contribution. They enable real-time collaboration and protect everyone's interests, ushering in a new era of digital creation.
01

GENERATIVE INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY SMART CONTRACTS

Generative IP smart contracts are advanced blockchain agreements. They incorporate source code, token supply, creators, data providers, and stakeholders to manage ownership and aspects of generative art NFTs. This enhances collaboration and fosters digital innovation.
02

Generative Art NFT

Generative Art NFTs', often addressed to art collectors and buyers, are unique digital tokens on the blockchain of a generative art piece. Each token comes with a unique serial number, representing a distinct variant produced by the Generative smart contract.

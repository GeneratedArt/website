Generative Tokenization

In the evolving landscape of generative art, decentralized digital contracts on the blockchain are key. These contracts facilitate collaboration among creators, data providers, and stakeholders, enabling real-time code and data contributions. This approach not only streamlines the production of significant art pieces but also ensures the protection of all parties' interests, marking a new era in digital art creation.
01

Generative Intellectual Property Tokens (GIPT)

Generative IP tokens are digital contracts on the blockchain, merging source code, algorithm creators, data providers, and stakeholders. They define ownership and manage render tokens, offering limited or unlimited supply, streamlining collaborations and fostering innovation in the digital realm.
02

Generative Render Tokens (GRT)

Generative render tokens, often addressed to art collectors and buyers, are unique digital tokens on the blockchain of a generative art piece. Each token comes with a unique serial number, representing a distinct variant produced by the Generative IP Tokens (GIPT).

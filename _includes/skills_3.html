P2P Creative Coding & Data Provisioning

A peer-to-peer blockchain platform enhances creative coding and data sharing, offering equitable recognition and strong protection. Digital contracts specify roles and contributions, and federated models maintain data security, improving project quality and collaboration.

Global Generative Art Exhibition Listing

The Global Generative Art Exhibition feature provides a platform for exploring and purchasing generative art worldwide. Users can navigate through cities using an intuitive app and buy physical artworks directly by scanning QR codes linked to crypto wallets. This feature seamlessly connects digital and physical art, offering a secure and convenient art buying experience.
